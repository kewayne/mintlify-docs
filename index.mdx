## What you can do with Shiipp Courier

Shiipp Courier is a full-featured backoffice platform built for courier companies. As a staff member or administrator, the dashboard gives you everything needed to run your operation:

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the backoffice">
    Navigate to your Shiipp backoffice URL and sign in with your staff credentials. See [Logging In](/getting-started/logging-in) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Operations Dashboard">
    Check today's KPIs — new sign-ups, pending packages, and outstanding invoices. See [Dashboard Overview](/getting-started/dashboard-overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process incoming packages">
    Use the [Packages](/operations/packages) page to record new arrivals, or head to [Check-Off & Sort](/operations/check-off) to scan barcodes from a manifest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run end-of-day finance">
    Close out the day with the [Point of Sale](/operations/pos) and then print the [End-of-Day Report](/operations/eod-report) for your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  New to Shiipp? Start with [Key Concepts](/concepts/package-lifecycle) to understand how packages move through the system before diving into daily operations.
</Tip>
