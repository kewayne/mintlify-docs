# Merging Customers

Occasionally, a customer might register multiple times or end up with duplicate accounts. The **Merge Customers** feature allows you to combine these accounts without losing tracking history or invoices.

## How to Merge
1. Navigate to **Operations > Customers**.
2. Select the duplicate customer account.
3. Click the **Merge** button.
4. Search for the primary account you want to merge into.
5. Confirm the action. 

<Warning>
This action is irreversible. All packages, invoices, and history from the merged account will be transferred to the primary account.
</Warning>
